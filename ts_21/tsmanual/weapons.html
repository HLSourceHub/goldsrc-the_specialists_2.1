Weapons


The cornerstone of any self-respecting first person shooter. Whatever your taste, there's something here to suit every Specialist.
You may find the weapons of TS a cut above that of your average game, in terms of both quality and quantity. Not convinced? Read on...

One of the most well-rounded SMGs, the MP5k combines a high rate of fire with good damage. It's not too heavy either, meaning you can still perform the full range of stunts whilst carrying it.

9x19mm Parabellum

30

Full-auto, Semi-auto & Burst

Perhaps one of the stealthiest of weapons available, the SD is virtually silent during firing, making surprise attacks much easier. It is one of the heavier SMGs though, meaning movement and stunting ability are limited.

Caliber

2.7x32mm

Magazine Capacity

40

Fire Modes

Full-auto, Semi-auto

Slots

Just hold the fire button and let rip, 40 rounds sprayed at your opponent are the result. The PDW has the highest magazine capacity of any of the SMGs, but for most effective usage, burst-firing is advised.


H&K UMP45

Caliber

.45 ACP

Magazine Capacity

25

Fire Modes

Full-auto, Semi-auto

Slots

25


The UMP combines a low, controllable rate of fire with the serious stopping power of .45 cal ammo. It's one of the heaviest of the SMGs, which results in your movement being inhibited.

IMI Mini-UZI

Caliber

Magazine Capacity

32

Fire Modes

Slots

20


The classic UZI is available here in all it's glory; rapid-fire and deadly at close range. As you might expect, there's a certain degree of recoil to be handled.

An assault rifle fit for all occasions, the M4 is an excellent all-round weapon. A high-rate of fire along with high damage combine to make this, in the right hands, a gun to be feared.

Kalasnikov AK-47

Caliber

7.62x39mm Soviet

Magazine Capacity

30

Fire Modes

Full-auto, Semi-auto

Slots

35


A weapon that needs no introduction, the AK47 is one of the most famous weapons in the world. With good reason, it's the largest calibre AR available in TS and packs a serious punch. With a scope, it can also be an accurate long range weapon.


Steyr AUG

Caliber

5.56x45 NATO

Magazine Capacity

30

Fire Modes

Full-auto, Semi-auto

Slots

40


Similar to the M4 in terms of use, though with a slightly slower fire-rate, the AUG is a more precise weapon. It is, however, still deadly at any range and a good all-round weapon.

Colt M16 A4

Caliber

5.56x45 NATO

Magazine Capacity

18

Fire Modes

Semi-auto, Burst

Slots

40

Vaguely related to the M4, the M16 is different in that it utilises single shot and burst fire modes only. It also has a lower magazine capacity, only 18 rounds. This is not as much of a disadvantage as it may seem, taking into consideration its good accuracy and high damage levels, it can be used as a light sniper rifle.

10" Steel

Fire Modes

Slash, Throw

Slots

1 (Maximum of 5)


A standard combat knife, which does heavy damage during close in fighting, and is usually lethal in one hit when thrown. However, due to its weight, the range of these thrown knives is limited.

Seal Knife

Caliber

6" Steel

Fire Modes

Slash, Throw

Slots

1 (Maximum of 10)


A lighter version of the combat knife which, whilst not doing as much damage in melee combat, can be thrown much further.

M61 Frag Grenade

You know what to do. Pop the pin and let her rip. Primary fire throws the grenade up and over obstacles, whilst secondary fires rolls it a shorter distance along the ground. As well as the explosive force, this weapon also sprays out shrapnel, which can injure and kill at longer ranges. It's available as a powerup in Deathmatch or for purchase in Teamplay.
